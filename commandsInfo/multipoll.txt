create a poll with up to five options, and a right answer to the question. 

if multiple right answers is specified, undefined behaviours will occur.

flags:
-r
the flag -r indicates the right answer and can be put behind any option

usage in chat:
/multipoll [question?], [option1], [option2], ..., [option5]

example:
/multipoll what is the capital of Sweden?, Malmo, Gothenburg, Luleå, Stockholm -r, Umeå
